The Centennial State

Socials

Colorado is known as the centennial state because it was the 38th state founded in 1876. Nestled against the Rocky Mountains the state is known for its towering mountains and the lifestyle that goes with proximity to them.

Exploring the front range and the mountains allows visitors to enjoy the great recreation mountain living offers in all four seasons. From hiking to backcountry snowboarding, there is something for everyone in this beautiful and scenic state. Contrary to what many think, the state is also known to have sunshine most days of the year due to its high elevation and dry climate. So, although it snows plenty during the winter months, it is easy to find that the sun is shining on most days.

Colorado is a wonderful state to be in. From the spacious cities to the mountain peaks with views for miles and miles. There is so much to do here in Colorado. No matter what someone decides to do and see, they are guaranteed to find the beauty Colorado offers.
